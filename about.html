About

Made iconic by the people performances that defined Luquillos, Horizon Hotel invites guests to experience an authentic celebration of Puerto Rico traditions. From its storied décor and inspired cuisine to renowned gatherings and lush poolscape, the hotel creates an atmosphere of celebration for locals and travelers alike.

The Experience

At Luquillos Horizon Hotel, every employee and associate you meet reflects the culture sharing stories, and traditional proverbs with guests who yearn to know more about Puerto Rican culture. Both guests and locals alike are drawn to the vibrant entertainment where famous performers have graced the stage. Unique decor from around the globe compliments enticing experiences on property, which have made the hotel a beloved gathering place.

Health & Safety

Following the most recent Puerto Rico’s Executive Order, it is a national mandate that all guests must be properly inoculated with any of the three vaccines authorized by the FDA (Janssen (J&J), Moderna or Pfizer). The vaccination requirement is applicable to any person staying 1 night or more at any hotel in Puerto Rico. If by any reason the guest can not provide the vaccination card or proof of vaccination, the guest is required to provide evidence of a COVID-19 test taken within 72 hours of checking in. The negative result must be from a qualified SARS-CoV2 viral test; either a nucleic acid amplification test ("NAAT") or an antigen test. For any questions or exceptions, please contact us directly. The use of face masks is also required, regardless of vaccination status. We appreciate your cooperation.
