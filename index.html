Welcome to Horizon Hotel

Get away with a memorable vacation

Enjoy a deligthful vacation in Hotel Santos where you can relax and enjoy our services

Horizon Hotel is infused in every experience, the lush of poolscape and uninterrupted stretch at the beach. Embark on adventures on the beach like surfing, volleyball or banana boat rides. Or, simply relax on a chaise beside the pool.

You won't have to stray far from your sun lounger to find a cool drink at this laidback Luquillos bar. Overlooking the pool deck, Horizon Hotel bar serves popular rum cocktails, and a varied menu of snacks, light meals, favorite sandwiches and pizzas. Order them as you sunbathe or sit in the shade under an umbrella.

Horizon Hotel pristine white sands and warm breezes offer the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Guests at Horizon Hotel can enjoy the ultimate getaway with 300 feet of secluded beachfront perfect for swimming, sand castles, or simply soaking up the sun.
